Airfryer Broccoli

Description

Have you tried making air fryer broccoli? All you need is olive oil, salt, and pepper, and the broccoli turns out wonderfully crispy and tasty.

Ingredients

  • 1 head fresh broccoli, cut into florets
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Combine broccoli, olive oil, salt, and pepper in a resealable bag. Set aside to allow the broccoli to soak up the oil, about 30 minutes.
  2. Preheat the air fryer to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  3. Place broccoli in the air fryer basket and cook until desired crispiness, 10 to 15 minutes, shaking air fryer basket halfway through cook.
